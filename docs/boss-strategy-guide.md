# Boss Strategy Guide

## Key Mechanic

Your angel's evolution stage matters in battle! Archangel (stage 3) and Seraphim (stage 4) deal **2 damage per shot** instead of 1. Max out your training to hit harder.

## Super Powers

Pick wisely after each correct answer:
- **Wings of Fire** (5 dmg) — burst damage, best when boss is low HP
- **Holy Lightning** (3 dmg + 2 heal) — balanced, always solid
- **Divine Shield** (blocks 2 hits) — buys time in long fights
- **Healing Potion** (restore 4 HP) — emergency survival

## Level 1: Shadow Wolf (HP: 15)

**Difficulty:** Easy
**Strategy:** Go aggressive. Pick Wings of Fire or Holy Lightning every time. You'll melt the wolf in under 10 seconds. Don't bother with shield or heal.

## Level 2: Frost Witch (HP: 20)

**Difficulty:** Medium
**Strategy:** Stay aggressive but pick Holy Lightning if you drop below 8 HP. The witch hits faster than the wolf. Keep shooting and the fatigue mechanic will slow her down.

## Level 3: Kraken Lord (HP: 25)

**Difficulty:** Hard
**Strategy:** This is the first real wall. Use Holy Lightning when above half HP (damage + heal), switch to Healing Potion when below 4 HP. The kraken hits hard but fatigues after 15 seconds — survive the initial onslaught and you'll overwhelm it.

**Key tip:** Max your angel to at least Archangel in training so you hit for 2 damage per shot.

## Level 4: Devil Queen (HP: 30)

**Difficulty:** Hard
**Strategy:** You MUST reach Archangel or Seraphim to win — 2 damage per shot is essential against 30 HP. Open with Wings of Fire to chunk her HP, then alternate Holy Lightning and Healing Potion based on your HP. Use Divine Shield if you're about to answer a hard question (shield blocks the penalty for wrong answers too).

**Key tip:** The boss fatigues over time. If you can survive past 20 seconds, you win.

## Level 5: Violet Avenger (HP: 40)

**Difficulty:** Very Hard
**Strategy:** Reach Seraphim (2 dmg/shot) or this is nearly impossible. Start with Wings of Fire for the burst, then play reactive: Healing Potion below 4 HP, Holy Lightning between 4-7 HP, Wings of Fire when you're comfortable. The boss attacks every 1.5 seconds initially but slows after 15s.

**Key tip:** Don't panic on wrong answers (2 damage penalty). Use Divine Shield before questions you're unsure about. Accuracy is more important than speed — a wrong answer costs 2 HP.

## General Tips

1. **Training matters** — reach Archangel/Seraphim for double damage shots
2. **Keep shooting** — mash that key/tap constantly, every shot counts
3. **Boss fatigues** — survive past 15 seconds and the boss slows down
4. **Heal early** — don't wait until 1 HP to heal, pick Healing Potion at 3-4 HP
5. **Power carry-over** — winning with high power means starting the next level stronger
